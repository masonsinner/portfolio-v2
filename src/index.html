About me

My fascination with technology began in my youth when I sought to optimize internet connectivity for lag-free online gaming. Recognized as the family "tech guy," I developed a reputation for troubleshooting and problem-solving. Despite my initial trajectory in the tech industry, I navigated a unique path. Post high school, I obtained an IT Support Certification from Google, securing a role as a Tier 1 IT Support Technician with the Department of Transportation. Simultaneously, I pursued self-taught development, gradually progressing through the ranks. Seeking a more guided approach, I undertook an apprenticeship with Coding Temple, delving into modern frameworks like React and TypeScript, and expanding my skills to backend development using SQL and Python's Flask library. This journey has equipped me with a versatile skill set and a resolute commitment to advancing in the technology field.

In addition to my tech journey, I'm a musician who enjoys performing live at venues in Houston and Los Angeles. Baseball remains a passion; I coach private lessons and play in local men's leagues. On the digital front, I still hold my own in online gaming. Looking ahead, my goals include assisting my younger brother in reaching the MLB. Eventually, I aspire to establish a tech repair business or agency to empower new grads and self-taught developers. Before that, I'm eager to contribute my skills and passion by working for industry giants like Microsoft, Google, or Apple.
